Qube::TextComponent

Allows a component to contain text or display text. Editing and display options are provided.

Constants

WRAP_BY_WORD

Wrap text by word (uses spaces or hyphens)

WRAP_BY_CHAR

Wrap text by characters

NO_LINE_WRAP

Do not wrap lines
